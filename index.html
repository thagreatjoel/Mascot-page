Meet Orphy ✨

Orphy is the friendly mascot of this Hack Club Swirl project. They represent curiosity, creativity, and the spirit of building together.

🌟 About Orphy

Orphy was created as part of a Hack Club Swirl project to bring more personality and fun into coding spaces. With big dreams and endless curiosity, Orphy symbolizes the joy of tinkering, experimenting, and sharing ideas with the community.
